# iCertify — AI‑Augmented Engineering Certification

Welcome to **iCertify**, a modern, AI‑native certification designed for professionals who want to demonstrate real capability in AI‑augmented engineering, governance, and responsible deployment.

This certification focuses on practical skill, reasoning quality, and the ability to design, evaluate, and operate AI systems safely and effectively.

---

## What You’ll Learn

### AI‑Augmented Engineering Foundations
- Understanding AI‑native workflows  
- Designing human‑in‑the‑loop systems  
- Evaluating model behavior and reasoning  

### Governance & Guardrails
- Control planes  
- Safety constraints  
- Risk‑aware deployment patterns  

### Practical Capability
- Prompt engineering  
- Evaluation frameworks  
- Real‑world scenario testing

## Why iCertify Exists

AI work today requires more than tool usage.  
It requires **judgment**, **governance**, **architecture**, and **evaluation discipline**.

iCertify is built to measure:
- How you think  
- How you reason  
- How you design safe systems  
- How you evaluate AI behavior  
- How you apply governance principles  

Not memorization.  
Not trivia.  
Actual capability.

---

## Certification Structure

### Level 1 — Foundations  
Demonstrates core understanding of AI‑augmented workflows and safe usage patterns.

### Level 2 — Applied Engineering  
Shows the ability to design, evaluate, and improve AI‑powered systems.

### Level 3 — Governance & Control Plane  
Validates advanced reasoning, guardrail design, and AI governance architecture.
